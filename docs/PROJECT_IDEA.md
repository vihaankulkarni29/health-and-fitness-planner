# Project Idea: Fitness Tracker

## 1. Introduction

The Fitness Tracker is a comprehensive web application designed to bridge the gap between personal fitness goals and tangible results. It provides a centralized platform for both trainees and trainers to manage workout programs, track progress, and foster a collaborative and effective fitness journey.

## 2. Target Audience

Our primary target audience is twofold:

*   **Trainees:** Individuals who are passionate about their fitness and are looking for a structured way to track their workouts, monitor their progress, and follow customized training programs. This includes everyone from beginners to experienced athletes.
*   **Trainers:** Fitness professionals who need an efficient tool to manage their clients, create personalized workout programs, and monitor their clients' progress and adherence to the programs.

## 3. Core Features

The application is built around a set of core features that cater to the needs of our target audience:

### 3.1. For Trainees

*   **Workout Logging:** Easily log completed workouts, including exercises, sets, reps, and weights.
*   **Program Management:** View and follow workout programs assigned by trainers or created by themselves.
*   **Health Metric Tracking:** Record and monitor key health metrics such as weight, body fat percentage, and measurements over time.
*   **Comprehensive Data Analysis:** Trainees can see a comprehensive data for their exercises, such as what are the main exercises that they do in a month/week. Major muscle group targetted wihtin a month/week, and all of that both available in numbers and graphs.
*   **Progress Visualization:** View historical data and progress through intuitive charts and graphs.
*   **Dashboard:** A personalized dashboard that provides a quick overview of the day's workout, recent activities, and progress highlights.

### 3.2. For Trainers

*   **Client Management:** Manage a roster of trainees, view their profiles, and track their overall progress. The trainer also has a proper sectioning to maintain readability for each of their clients
*   **Program Creation:** A powerful and flexible tool to create customized workout programs with specific exercises, sets, reps, and instructions.
*   **Client Progress Monitoring:** Monitor clients' workout logs, health metrics, and adherence to their assigned programs. This includes access to the same comprehensive data analysis and visualizations available to the trainee.
*   **Secure Communication:** (Future Feature) A messaging system to communicate with clients directly within the application.

## 4. Technology Stack

The application is built using a modern and robust technology stack:

*   **Backend:**
    *   **Framework:** FastAPI (Python)
    *   **Database:** PostgreSQL
    *   **ORM:** SQLAlchemy
    *   **Authentication:** JWT (JSON Web Tokens)
*   **Frontend:**
    *   **Framework:** React.js
    *   **Styling:** CSS (with potential for a component library like Material-UI or Bootstrap)
*   **Deployment:** Docker

## 5. Vision and Future Scope

Our long-term vision is to evolve the Fitness Tracker into a holistic health and wellness platform, with the ultimate goal of transitioning to a mobile-first experience. Future enhancements may include:

*   **Nutrition Tracking:** Integrating a food diary and nutritional analysis.
*   **Social Features:** Allowing users to connect with friends, share progress, and participate in challenges.
*   **Advanced Analytics:** Providing deeper insights and predictive analytics based on user data.
*   **Mobile Application:** The final goal is to make this a mobile application. Native mobile apps for iOS and Android for a seamless on-the-go experience will be the primary focus in the long run.
*   **Wearable Device Integration:** Syncing data from popular fitness trackers and smartwatches.
